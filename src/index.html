Quintessential Quandts

In case you haven't heard, they met in a bar. Romantic, right??

It was coincidence that either of them were there in the first place. Claire was catching up with a friend from high school. Nick had been out to dinner in Milwaukee just a few hours earlier when he and his friend decided to drive to Madison on a whim. After what felt like an hour of standing in line outside of Whiskey Jack's, the bouncer let both groups know that they didn't have any tables for just two people. He offered to seat both pairs at one table. They hesitantly agreed (stranger danger!), but after semi-awkward introductions and a few drinks each, the conversation started to flow! Throughout the course of the night, Claire and Nick couldn't help but notice a magnetism between them.

Their first date was a four hour car ride with no real destination. They talked, they laughed, they listened, and they sat in silence. As the hours passed, they both began to realize that this was something neither of them had ever felt before.

Three weeks later, Nick asked Claire what type of wedding venue she wanted. She didn't know, so he showed her Anthony Chapel, and she decided that was where she was going to get married.

Three months later, Nick asked Claire if she wanted to spend the rest of their lives together. You guessed it - SHE SAID YES!!
